Why Blood Donation Matters in Ethiopia

Ethiopia faces a chronic blood shortage with only 40% of the required blood supply available annually. Every day, patients in need of blood transfusions face life-threatening situations due to this shortage.

Maternal Health

25% of maternal deaths in Ethiopia are related to blood loss during childbirth.

Accidents & Trauma

Road accidents claim thousands of lives annually where blood could save many.

Child Health

Children with severe anemia from malaria often require urgent blood transfusions.

Community Impact

Your donation can save up to 3 lives

Ready to Donate?

Join thousands of Ethiopians who donate blood regularly. The process is safe, simple, and saves lives.

1. Check Eligibility

You must be 18-65 years old, weigh at least 50kg, and be in good health. No tattoos/piercings in last 6 months.

2. Find a Center

Locate the nearest blood donation center. We have 27 centers across Ethiopia with trained staff.

3. Donate & Save Lives

The donation takes about 10 minutes. You'll get refreshments and can donate again in 3 months.

Blood Type Compatibility

Not all blood types are compatible. Know which types can donate to and receive from others.

O- is the universal donor (can donate to all blood types) while AB+ is the universal recipient (can receive from all blood types).
